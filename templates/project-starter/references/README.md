# References

Downloaded source material — papers, reports, exported data, and offline copies of references.

Track all references in `docs/references.md` (title, source, URL, what it is used for).

## What Goes Here

- Downloaded papers or reports (PDF)
- Exported data files (CSV, JSON)
- Screenshots of source material that may move or disappear
- Offline copies of any reference the presentation depends on

## What Goes in docs/references.md

The index — every reference tracked with title, source, URL, and which claim or slide it supports.
